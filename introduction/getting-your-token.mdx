---
title: Getting Your Token
---


To authenticate your API requests, you need to obtain an API token. Follow the steps below to get your token:

## Step 1: Log in to Your Account

1. Navigate to the [Login Page](https://fintech-portal.mgrp.mobi/login).
2. Enter your credentials and log in to your account. 
<img height="200" src="/images/login-page.png" />

## Step 2: Access the API Settings

1. Once logged in, go to your account dashboard.
2. Click on **Settings** in the navigation menu.
3. Select **API Tokens** from the settings options.
<img height="200" src="/images/dashboard.png" />

## Step 3: Generate a New Token

1. Click the **Generate Token** button.
2. Provide a name for your token (e.g., "MyAppToken") for easy identification.
3. Set the appropriate permissions for the token based on your needs.
4. Click **Create Token**.

## Step 4: Copy and Save Your Token

- Once the token is generated, copy it immediately.
- Save it securely in a password manager or environment variable. **Do not share your token** as it provides access to your account.

## Step 5: Use the Token in Your API Requests

Include the token in the `Authorization` header of your API requests:
<img height="200" src="/images/request.png" />
